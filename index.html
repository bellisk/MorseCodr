MorseCodr

MorseCodr is a program to practise communicating with Morse code.

You can connect to other users and send Morse signals that are automatically interpreted by the program. Once you become more proficient, you can reduce the amount of display and interpretation provided.

  • Morse code tape display
  • Dot/dash colouring
  • Letter interpretation
  • Adjustable speed
Source. Issue tracker. By Rachel Knowler and David Stark.
